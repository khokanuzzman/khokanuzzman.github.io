Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the privacy policy of SafeKid App Control. Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we handle data and what permissions we use, all while keeping your information secure and local to your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. All data is handled locally within your device and is used only to provide parental control features.

Data Usage

  • Installed app data is used to let you select which apps to lock for supervision.
  • App usage is monitored via Accessibility Service only to apply lock screens.
  • Photos taken during intruder capture are stored securely in private app storage.
  • User name and email (if provided) are stored only on your device for personalization.

Permissions

  • Accessibility Service: Required to detect which apps are launched to enforce locks.
  • Camera: Required to take photos of unauthorized access attempts (if enabled by the user).
  • Storage: Used to save intruder photos and gallery items securely inside the app.

Third-Party Services

SafeKid App Control does not use any third-party analytics or advertising tools that collect personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. All updates will be posted on this page. We recommend reviewing this policy regularly.
